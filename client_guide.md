## How It Works

**Step 1 — Scan**
Every minute, the bot scans your watchlist across multiple timeframes, looking for trades that meet your criteria. It checks price momentum, volume, trend direction, and more — simultaneously, instantly, without fatigue.

**Step 2 — Decide**
Before firing any trade, the bot requires at least two independent signals to agree. No single indicator can trigger a trade alone. It's built to filter out noise and only act on conviction.

**Step 3 — Execute**
The moment a setup qualifies, the bot places the trade with a stop-loss and a profit target already locked in. The risk is defined before the trade even opens. No guessing, no adjusting mid-trade under pressure.

## Risk Controls

The platform is built with multiple layers of protection:

- **Daily loss limit** — the bot stops trading automatically if losses hit your set threshold for the day
- **Max open positions** — never too much exposure at once
- **Per-trade risk cap** — each trade risks only a defined percentage of your account
- **Consecutive loss protection** — the bot pauses itself after a losing streak and waits for your review
- **Symbol restrictions** — trade only the assets you approve, nothing else
- **Kill switch** — stop everything immediately, at any time, with one click
- **Paper mode** — run it without real money until you're confident
